Frequently Asked Questions

How do instant payouts work for walkers?

Walkers receive payments directly to their bank account within 24 hours of completing a walk. No waiting for weekly or monthly payouts.

Are there really no fees for dog owners?

Yes! Dog owners can book walks completely free. We charge walkers a flat monthly or yearly subscription instead of taking a percentage of their earnings.

What happens if I need to cancel?

You can cancel your subscription at any time from your profile settings. You'll retain access until the end of your billing period.

Is my dog insured during walks?

Yes, all walks include insurance coverage for peace of mind. Our walkers are also bonded and background checked.
